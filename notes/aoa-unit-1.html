Unit 2: Background and Divide and Conquer Method 1. Background 1.1 Review of Algorithm An algorithm is a step-by-step process or set of rules to solve a specific problem. Characteristics of an Algorithm: Input: It can take zero or more inputs. Output: It produces at least one output. Definiteness: Each step is precisely defined. Finiteness: It terminates after a finite number of steps. Effectiveness: Each operation must be basic enough to be carried out.
